Screening Parameters Guide

Parameter categories

  • Demographics: Sex at birth, age, and education level.
  • Lifestyle: Current smoking status and cigarettes per day.
  • Medical history: Blood pressure medication, prior stroke, hypertension, and diabetes.
  • Vitals and labs: Cholesterol, systolic and diastolic blood pressure, BMI, heart rate, and glucose.
