Screening Parameters Guide

Input definitions

  • Gender: Encoded for the underlying model as 0 for female and 1 for male.
  • Hemoglobin: Oxygen-carrying protein level in blood; lower values often suggest anemia.
  • MCH: Mean Corpuscular Hemoglobin, or average hemoglobin per red blood cell.
  • MCHC: Mean Corpuscular Hemoglobin Concentration inside red blood cells.
  • MCV: Mean Corpuscular Volume, describing average red blood cell size.
