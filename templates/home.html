How the workflow works

  1. Select the screening module that matches the condition you want to review.
  2. Enter realistic values from recent vitals or lab work where possible.
  3. Submit the form to receive a model-estimated risk result and supporting guidance.

  • Educational disclaimers keep the product grounded and credible.
