Screening Parameters Guide

Input definitions

  • Pregnancies: Number of pregnancies recorded in the source dataset.
  • Glucose: Plasma glucose concentration, one of the strongest diabetes-related risk signals.
  • Blood Pressure: Diastolic blood pressure value in mmHg.
  • Skin Thickness: Triceps skin-fold thickness, often used as a body composition indicator.
  • Insulin: Two-hour serum insulin concentration.
  • BMI: Body Mass Index, commonly associated with metabolic risk.
  • Diabetes Pedigree Function: Family predisposition score used in the original dataset.
  • Age: Age in years.
